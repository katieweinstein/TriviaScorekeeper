# Privacy Policy for Trivia Scorekeeper

*Last updated: February 1, 2026*

Katie Weinstein (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the Trivia Scorekeeper mobile application (the “App”).

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

## Information We Collect

Trivia Scorekeeper does not collect any personal data.

The App does not collect, transmit, store, or share any information externally, including but not limited to:

Names

Email addresses

Device identifiers

Location data

Usage analytics

Crash reports

Advertising or tracking data

## Local Data Storage

The App allows users to enter player names for scoring purposes.
This information:

Is stored locally on your device only

Is saved using SQLite on the device

Is never transmitted off the device

Is not accessible to the developer

Deleting the App will remove all locally stored data.

## Third-Party Services

Trivia Scorekeeper does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking technologies.

## Children’s Privacy

Trivia Scorekeeper is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13, but it is suitable for users of all ages.
Because the App does not collect any personal data, it does not knowingly collect information from children or adults.

## Data Security

Because all data remains on your device and is not transmitted or stored externally, no external data security measures are required.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

If the App’s functionality changes in a way that affects user privacy, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
